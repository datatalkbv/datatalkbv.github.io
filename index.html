The DataTalk Story

DataTalk was founded by Thomas van Turnhout and Koen van Eijk, two lifelong friends with a shared passion for innovation. After successful corporate careers and the establishment of their first venture, Oakwood Technologies, they embarked on a new journey to transform the technology landscape with specialized AI solutions.

With their groundbreaking Smart Robotic Process Automation software suite, Automagica, and other solutions, Thomas and Koen served high-profile companies across various industries. Their success led to the acquisition of Oakwood Technologies by Netcall plc, where they continued to drive innovation for another three years.
